---Maze Game---

---Objective---

The objective of the game is to get 200 points 
and leave the maze at the end of level 2 to go
to go forward a level you go to the bottom left
of the screen and to go back to level 1 you go
to the top left of the level

---How to Play---

My game is controlled by the arrow keys. 
You recieve 10 points by collecting a pellet.
